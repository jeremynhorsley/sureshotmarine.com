What I help with

Charging & Batteries Diagnostics

Alternators, regulators, shore chargers, battery banks, and mystery drains while docked.

Shore Power & AC Safety ABYC-minded

Grounding/bonding sanity checks, nuisance trips, inlet issues, and safe, tidy corrections.

NMEA 2000 / Electronics Network

Device dropouts, backbone power issues, interference noise, and clean network layout fixes.

Corrosion & “Spaghetti Wiring” Cleanup

Tracing unknown circuits, labeling, improving terminations, and making systems readable again.

Common problems I’m called for

  • Batteries not charging correctly, or charging inconsistently
  • Parasitic drain killing batteries in a few days
  • Random breaker trips, shutdowns, or “it worked yesterday” faults
  • Bilge pump / float switch not working reliably
  • NMEA 2000 devices disappearing or reading bad data
  • Corroded terminals, overheated connections, and failed crimps
  • Previous-owner wiring with no labels or documentation
