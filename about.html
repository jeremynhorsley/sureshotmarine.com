About

Technical, documented troubleshooting.

Sure Shot Marine is built around structured diagnostics: measured data, defined test procedures, and clear documentation.

Background

My background is in commissioning and field diagnostics for industrial energy systems. That work requires disciplined testing, traceable results, and clear communication. I apply the same approach to marine electrical and onboard systems.

What to expect

Defined scope before work starts

We start by clarifying symptoms, system configuration, and operating conditions.

Measured testing repeatable

Testing focuses on isolating the cause, not swapping parts. Findings are evidence-based.

Clear documentation usable

Notes and results are written so you can act on them or share with a yard/installer.

Practical next steps priority

Recommendations are prioritized by safety, reliability, and effort/cost.

Service area

San Francisco Bay Area marinas and nearby locations. If you’re outside the area, email with details and I’ll let you know what’s realistic.
