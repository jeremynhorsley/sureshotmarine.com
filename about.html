About

I operate Horsley Marine Electrical and perform all diagnostic and corrective work personally. My focus is structured marine electrical troubleshooting and onboard systems diagnostics for sailboats and powerboats in the San Francisco Bay Area.

Background

I hold a Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering and have prior service in the United States Navy and United States Coast Guard working with electronic and communication systems.

My professional experience includes electrical systems commissioning, structured fault isolation, measurement-based diagnostics, and technical documentation in industrial and marine environments.

Technical Scope

  • DC and AC fault isolation
  • Charging and battery system diagnostics
  • NMEA 2000 network troubleshooting
  • Shore power and distribution evaluation (scope-dependent)
  • Bilge and critical circuit verification
  • System mapping and documentation

Method

  • Verify reported condition
  • Measure and isolate affected circuit
  • Identify root cause
  • Correct using appropriate protection and routing
  • Document findings where applicable

Service Area

I am based in the East Bay and serve Alameda, Oakland, Berkeley, Richmond, Sausalito, and surrounding San Francisco Bay Area marinas.

Scheduling

Work is scheduled by appointment. Initial intake requires marina location, description of the issue, and clear photographs of affected areas. Text communication is preferred.
