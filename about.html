About

Sure Shot Marine LLC | Troubleshooting since 1995.

Background

Sure Shot Marine provides marine electrical diagnostics and repair for sailboats, yachts, and commercial vessels in the San Francisco Bay Area.

I'm Jeremyn Horsley — Navy veteran avionics tech, Coast Guard electronics technician, and electrical engineer with nearly three decades of hands-on experience in complex electrical and mechanical systems. I know boats, I know electrical systems, and I know how to find what's wrong and fix it right.

I'm an ABYC member and perform all work to ABYC standards.

What I Work On

  • DC and AC fault isolation
  • Charging and battery system diagnostics
  • Shore power and distribution evaluation
  • Stray current and corrosion testing
  • NMEA 2000 network troubleshooting
  • Bilge and critical circuit verification
  • System mapping and documentation
