About

My name is Jeremyn Horsley, and I operate Sure Shot Marine. I've been working with electrical and electronic systems in marine environments since 1995 — and providing marine electrical services in the Bay Area since 2012.

My work focuses on the problems that are hard to pin down — charging systems that behave inconsistently, unexplained battery drain, intermittent faults, and wiring that has been modified and re-modified over decades. I own a 1990 Ericson 32-200 and have been sailing in the Bay Area for years.

Background

I have a B.S. in Electrical Engineering and spent several years as a U.S. Coast Guard electronics technician — maintaining 47' motor lifeboats, serving aboard a cutter, and supporting Rescue21 and station boat electronics across the district. Before that, U.S. Navy avionics aboard the USS Enterprise, and civilian work troubleshooting industrial power systems.

Technical Scope

  • DC and AC fault isolation
  • Charging and battery system diagnostics
  • NMEA 2000 network troubleshooting
  • Shore power and distribution evaluation (scope-dependent)
  • Bilge and critical circuit verification
  • System mapping and documentation

Method

  • Verify reported condition
  • Measure and isolate affected circuit
  • Identify root cause
  • Correct using appropriate protection and routing
  • Document findings where applicable

Service Area

I am based in the East Bay and serve Alameda, Oakland, Berkeley, Richmond, Sausalito, and surrounding San Francisco Bay Area marinas.

Scheduling

Work is scheduled by appointment. Initial intake requires marina location, description of the issue, and clear photographs of affected areas. Text communication is preferred.
