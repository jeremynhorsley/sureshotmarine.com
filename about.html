About

Practical electrical troubleshooting for working boats.

I work on marine electrical and onboard systems in the San Francisco Bay Area. My focus is straightforward: measure what’s happening, isolate the cause, and document clear next steps.

Background

I hold a Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering and have experience working on electrical, navigation, and communication systems in military and industrial environments.

My professional work has involved diagnosing and commissioning high-reliability power systems, including AC and DC distribution, protection systems, and fault analysis. The same disciplined approach applies on boats — smaller systems, but often just as complex.

How I approach marine systems

  • Start with measurements, not assumptions
  • Look for root cause, not just symptoms
  • Verify fixes under real operating load
  • Keep documentation simple and useful

Credentials & Training

  • Bachelor of Science — Electrical Engineering
  • U.S. Navy & Coast Guard electrical systems experience
  • OSHA 10 Hour Safety Training
  • NFPA 70E Electrical Safety Training

Scope

I focus on diagnostics and system evaluation. If a project requires extensive rewiring, fiberglass work, engine mechanical repair, or haul-out yard coordination, I’m happy to recommend appropriate specialists.
