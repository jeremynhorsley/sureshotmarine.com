Sure Shot Marine is named after my 1990 Ericson 32-200, S/V Sure Shot. The boat came with the name. It stuck

Born on the coast of the Pacific northwest, I grew up on the water. Joined the Navy, learned electronics, served aboard USS Enterprise. The Coast Guard brought me back to the west coast in 2009, and I've been working on private vessels in the Bay Area since 2012.

Boats live a hard life. Salt air, bilge water, vibration, heat cycles, and years of well-meaning modifications stack up on every system aboard. The fault is rarely obvious. It might be a corroded wire, a bad connection, or something buried three systems deep. Finding it is the work, and when I do, I'll tell you exactly what it is, what caused it, and what it takes to fix it.
