About Sure Shot Marine

I’m a marine electrician based in the San Francisco Bay Area. I work on sailboats and powerboats throughout Alameda, Berkeley, Oakland, and surrounding marinas.

My background is in electrical engineering and mission-critical power systems. Before focusing on boats, I worked on large energy systems where reliability mattered. That experience carries over directly to marine electrical work.

On boats, small wiring problems turn into big headaches. My job is to track down the actual cause and fix it properly — not guess and not replace parts blindly.

Experience

  • Bachelor of Science – Electrical Engineering
  • U.S. Navy & Coast Guard electrical systems
  • Energy systems commissioning and diagnostics
  • AC/DC power distribution and fault troubleshooting
  • NFPA 70E trained

What You Can Expect

  • Clear communication
  • Methodical troubleshooting
  • Clean, organized work
  • Practical recommendations
  • No unnecessary upselling

If you want someone who will take the time to understand your system and fix it correctly, that’s what I focus on.
