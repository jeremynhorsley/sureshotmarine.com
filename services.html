Services

Electrical Troubleshooting

Intermittent faults, dead circuits, breaker trips, voltage drop, parasitic drain, and corrosion-related failures.

Charging & Battery Systems

Alternators, regulators, shore chargers, DC-DC charging, battery banks, and charging performance verification.

Lithium Battery Conversions

LiFePO4 system design, charging source compatibility, BMS integration, alternator protection, and conversion from lead-acid with proper system verification.

Shore Power & AC Systems

Inlet and distribution faults, nuisance trips, GFCI and galvanic protection verification, and grounding and bonding evaluation.

Corrosion & Stray Current Diagnostics

Hull potential measurement, stray current testing, galvanic isolator verification, and bonding system evaluation using a silver/silver chloride reference electrode.

Power Quality Analysis

Shore power voltage and frequency verification, harmonic distortion, voltage drop under load, and identification of power quality issues affecting sensitive electronics and charging systems.

Thermal Imaging

Infrared inspection of panels, connections, and wiring under load. Identifies hot spots, failing terminations, and overloaded circuits before they become failures.

NMEA 2000 & Electronics

Backbone power issues, terminations, topology errors, device dropouts, and network voltage verification.

Bilge & Critical Circuits

Bilge pumps, float switches, alarms, auto/manual logic, feed integrity, and corrective wiring work.

Wiring Cleanup & Documentation

Circuit tracing, labeling, termination upgrades, routing improvements, and correction of unsafe modifications.

System Mapping

Panel circuit index, battery and charging path documentation, shore power path, and annotated photo set delivered as a PDF.

Communications & Navigation Systems

VHF, AIS, SSB, and GPS antenna system testing, feedline integrity, radio installation and verification, chartplotter integration, and diagnosis of range or performance issues.
