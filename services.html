What I work on

Electrical fault isolation 12/24/48V DC

Breaker trips, intermittent loss of power, unexplained shutdowns, voltage drop, bad connections, corrosion issues.

Battery drain & charging problems diagnostics

Parasitic draw, undercharging, overcharging, alternator/charger behavior, regulator setup, protection and wiring checks.

Inverter / shore power issues AC

Inverter cutouts, shore power nuisance trips, transfer switch problems, grounding/bonding checks (scope-dependent).

NMEA 2000 troubleshooting network

Backbone power and voltage drop, termination, device dropouts, data issues, connector/cable faults.

Bilge & safety circuits verification

Bilge pumps, float switches, alarms, auto/manual logic, power feed integrity, wiring quality checks.

Pre-purchase electrical check independent

Targeted electrical condition assessment with a written list of findings to support your decision or negotiation.

Common service calls

  • Batteries dead after a few days at the dock
  • Alternator not charging (or charging inconsistently)
  • Electronics reboot when starting the engine or under load
  • Random breaker trips or “sometimes it works” circuits
  • NMEA 2000 devices disappearing or showing errors
  • Bilge pump runs but doesn’t clear water (or never turns on)

How the visit typically goes

  • Quick symptom review and system overview (what’s installed, what changed, when it started)
  • Baseline measurements and visual inspection of likely failure points
  • Targeted tests to isolate the fault (under load when needed)
  • Short written summary: what was found + recommended fixes (prioritized)

What I need from you

  • Boat make/model and marina location
  • A short description of the problem and when it happens
  • Anything that changed recently (new batteries, new electronics, recent yard work, etc.)
  • Access plan (keys/lockbox, dock gate, shore power availability)

How billing works

  • Most work is billed hourly with a minimum site visit
  • If the problem is clearly defined, I can usually give a rough range before arrival
  • Parts are billed separately (if you want me to source them)
