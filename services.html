Common service calls

  • Batteries dead after a few days at the dock
  • Alternator not charging (or charging inconsistently)
  • Electronics reboot when starting the engine or under load
  • Random breaker trips or “sometimes it works” circuits
  • NMEA 2000 devices disappearing or showing errors
  • Bilge pump runs but doesn’t clear water (or never turns on)

How a service visit typically goes

  • Quick symptom review and system overview (what’s installed, what changed, when it started)
  • Baseline measurements and visual inspection of likely failure points
  • Targeted tests to isolate the fault (under load when needed)
  • Short written summary: what was found + recommended fixes (prioritized)

What I need from you

  • Boat make/model and marina location
  • A short description of the problem and when it happens
  • Anything that changed recently (new batteries, new electronics, recent yard work, etc.)
  • Access plan (keys/lockbox, dock gate, shore power availability)
