Service Categories

Electrical Troubleshooting

Intermittent faults, dead circuits, breaker trips, voltage drop, parasitic drain, and corrosion-related failures.

Charging & Battery Systems

Alternators, regulators, shore chargers, DC-DC charging, battery banks, and charging performance verification.

Shore Power & AC Systems

Inlet and distribution faults, nuisance trips, protection verification, and grounding/bonding evaluation (scope-dependent).

NMEA 2000 & Electronics

Backbone power issues, terminations, topology errors, device dropouts, and network voltage verification.

Bilge & Critical Circuits

Bilge pumps, float switches, alarms, auto/manual logic, feed integrity, and corrective wiring work.

Wiring Cleanup & System Clarity

Circuit tracing, labeling, termination upgrades, routing improvements, and correction of unsafe modifications.

Electrical System Mapping

Panel circuit tracing, battery routing clarification, charging path identification, and simplified owner documentation.

Solar Planning & Integration

Array sizing, controller selection, wiring layout, protection planning, and integration with existing charging systems.
