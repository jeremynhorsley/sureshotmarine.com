Core services

Electrical Troubleshooting Diagnostics

Structured fault isolation for intermittent issues, dead circuits, nuisance trips, and “it worked yesterday” problems. Verify → measure → isolate → confirm root cause.

Charging & Battery Systems 12V/24V

Alternators, regulators, DC-DC chargers, shore chargers, battery bank performance, and parasitic drain diagnosis. Clean corrections and clear explanations.

Shore Power & AC Safety Safety

Inlet and breaker issues, AC grounding/bonding sanity checks, nuisance trips, and safe corrections. Emphasis on tidy routing and proper protection.

NMEA 2000 & Electronics Network

Backbone power problems, device dropouts, noisy installs, and troubleshooting of network layout issues. Practical solutions that keep gear online reliably.

Bilge Pump & Critical Circuits Reliability

Bilge pump faults, float switches, alarms, and critical safety circuits. Repairs prioritize corrosion resistance, strain relief, and reliability.

Wiring Cleanup & System Clarity Organization

Tracing unknown circuits, improving terminations, labeling, and making systems understandable again. Especially useful after years of owner upgrades and yard changes.

Pre-survey preparation

If you’re getting ready for a marine survey (sale or insurance), I can help reduce common electrical findings and improve system clarity before the surveyor steps onboard.

  • Identify and correct obvious safety issues (overfused circuits, overheated connections, poor terminations)
  • Verify bilge pump function and critical circuits
  • Improve labeling and documentation for panel/circuits
  • Clean up battery/charging wiring and common corrosion points
  • Provide a short summary of what was corrected and what should be planned next

Upgrades & planning

I can help you plan upgrades so they’re clean, safe, and maintainable — and so you don’t end up with a “patchwork” system. For larger projects, a short planning visit often saves time and money.

Solar / Charging Upgrades Planning

High-level design guidance for solar, charging sources, and battery bank changes. Focused on safe wiring paths, protection, and realistic performance.

System Mapping Documentation

A practical “owner map” of what’s installed and how it’s wired. Ideal for new boat owners or anyone inheriting years of unlabeled changes.

What I don’t do

I’m focused on electrical troubleshooting and clean corrections. If your job requires major fiberglass work, engine internal repairs, or haul-out yard operations, I’ll tell you early and can coordinate with the right provider.

If you’re not sure whether your issue is electrical or mechanical, send a short description and photos — I’ll help you sort it quickly.
