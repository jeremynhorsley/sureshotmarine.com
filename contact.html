Service inquiries

Sure Shot Marine provides structured electrical diagnostics and system design services throughout the San Francisco Bay Area.

To keep scheduling efficient, please include the following in your email:

  • Vessel make and model
  • Marina location
  • Clear description of the concern (what is happening and when)
  • Recent changes (batteries, chargers, electronics, wiring)
  • Preferred contact number

New boat owners

If you recently purchased a vessel and would like a full electrical system review or mapping session, mention that in your message. A structured overview can help clarify charging architecture, circuit protection, and system condition before upgrades are made.

Availability

Work is scheduled by appointment. Most new clients begin with a diagnostic session.
